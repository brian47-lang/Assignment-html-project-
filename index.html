Profile

Brian is a 27-year-old Kenyan pursuing an Honors course in Business Information System at the International University of Management in Namibia.
In his final year, he has developed a passion for tech and aspires to grow in the industry, particularly in app and web development.
While Brian is a bit introverted, those close to him know that he is a warm and welcoming person who enjoys spending time with family and friends.
Outside of his studies, Brian loves to travel, cook, play video games, and playing football.
He cherishes his family deeply, including his older brother Samuel and his son Adrian, as well as his parents.
Brian's desire to learn and grow, coupled with his affable nature, makes him an excellent candidate for any team.
